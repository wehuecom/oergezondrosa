# SMS Lead Response Playbook

Proven SMS conversation flows for converting ad leads into booked appointments. Use these patterns with `/respond-to-leads`.

## Speed to Lead Rules

1. **Respond within 5 minutes** — conversion drops 80% after 5 minutes
2. **Always ask a question** — keep the conversation alive
3. **Sound human** — no "Dear valued customer" or corporate tone
4. **Use their name** — personalization converts
5. **Max 2-4 sentences per message** — SMS is not email

## Conversation Flows

### Flow 1: Fresh Lead (Just Opted In)

**Message 1 — Instant response:**
```
Hey {name}! This is {your_name} from {business}.
Saw you were interested in {offer}. Quick question —
what specifically caught your eye?
```

**If they reply with a question:**
```
Great question! {answer their question in 1-2 sentences}.
Would you be open to a quick 10-min call so I can learn
more about your situation? I have time {day} at {time}
or {day} at {time} — what works?
```

**If they reply with interest:**
```
Awesome! Sounds like we'd be a great fit. Let me ask —
{qualifying question about their situation}?
```

**If they reply with objection:**
```
Totally get that. {address objection briefly}. Most of our
{clients/customers} felt the same way before they saw
{proof/result}. Want to see some examples?
```

### Flow 2: No Response After 24 Hours

**Follow-up 1 (24h):**
```
Hey {name}! Just following up — did you get a chance
to think about {offer}? Happy to answer any questions.
```

**Follow-up 2 (48h):**
```
{name} — quick check-in. I know you were looking at
{offer}. I actually just {relevant update/result/opening}.
Still interested?
```

**Follow-up 3 (72h — last attempt):**
```
Last message from me — don't want to bug you! If
{offer} isn't the right fit right now, no worries at all.
Just reply "stop" and I'll remove you, or "yes" if
you want to chat. Either way, wishing you the best!
```

### Flow 3: Booking the Appointment

**After qualifying:**
```
Love it — I think we can definitely help with that.
Let's hop on a quick call so I can map out exactly
what we'd do. Here's my calendar: {link}

What day works best — {day} or {day}?
```

**If they don't book from link:**
```
No worries if the link is tricky — how about {day}
at {time}? I'll call you directly at this number.
```

**After booking:**
```
You're all set for {day} at {time}! I'll {call you / send
a Zoom link} 5 min before. Looking forward to it, {name}.
```

### Flow 4: Appointment Reminders

**24 hours before:**
```
Hey {name}! Just a heads up — we've got our call
tomorrow at {time}. Still good for you?
```

**1 hour before:**
```
Hey {name}! We're on in 1 hour at {time}. Here's the
link: {zoom_link}. See you soon!
```

### Flow 5: No-Show Recovery

**Immediately after no-show:**
```
Hey {name}! Looks like we missed each other — no
worries at all, things come up. Want to reschedule?
I've got {day} or {day} open this week.
```

**24h after no-show:**
```
Hey {name} — still want to connect? I kept a slot
open for you on {day}. If not, totally understand.
Just let me know either way!
```

### Flow 6: Post-Appointment Follow-Up

**After a great call:**
```
{name} — awesome chatting with you! As discussed,
here's what we'll do:

1. {next step 1}
2. {next step 2}
3. {next step 3}

I'll {action} by {date}. Any questions in the meantime,
just text me!
```

**After a "let me think about it":**
```
{name} — great talking today! Take your time on the
decision. One thing I forgot to mention — {compelling
detail or case study}. Let me know if any questions
come up!
```

## Qualifying Questions by Industry

### Service Businesses (Barbershops, Salons, etc.)
- How often do you typically come in?
- What's your biggest frustration with your current [barber/stylist/provider]?
- How far are you willing to travel for a great experience?

### Marketing / Agency Clients
- What's your current monthly revenue?
- How are you getting customers right now?
- Have you run ads before? What happened?
- What would [20/50/100] new leads per month do for your business?

### E-commerce
- What products are you most interested in?
- Have you tried similar products before?
- What's been your biggest challenge with [category]?

### Professional Services (Coaches, Consultants, etc.)
- What's the main thing you're trying to achieve in the next 90 days?
- Have you worked with a [coach/consultant] before?
- What's holding you back right now?

## Disqualification Signals

Know when to stop pursuing:
- **"Just looking"** after 3+ messages — move to nurture, stop hard selling
- **Wrong budget** — if they can't afford it, be honest and redirect
- **Wrong location** — for local businesses, too far away
- **Competitor shopping** — they mention pricing from 5 competitors
- **Unresponsive after 3 follow-ups** — archive and move on

## Tone Guide

**DO:**
- Use casual, friendly language
- Use contractions (don't, won't, can't)
- Ask questions
- Be genuine
- Match their energy (if they're excited, be excited)

**DON'T:**
- Use emojis excessively (1-2 max per message)
- Send walls of text
- Be pushy or desperate
- Use salesy language ("limited time offer!", "act now!")
- Send more than 3 follow-ups without a response
