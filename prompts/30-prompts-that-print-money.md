# 30 Prompts That Print Money

These aren't generic ChatGPT prompts. These work because Claude already knows your business, your audience, your voice, and your tools (thanks to your CLAUDE.md). Every response will be personalized to YOU.

Copy-paste any of these. Watch what happens.

---

## Content Ideas (get unstuck in 10 seconds)

1. **Give me 10 content ideas my audience actually cares about right now.** Not generic stuff — things that would make them stop scrolling and say "this is exactly what I needed to hear."

2. **What are the 5 biggest objections my audience has about buying from me?** Turn each one into a piece of content that destroys the objection.

3. **Write me a "myth vs reality" post about my industry.** Something that would make my audience share it and say "FINALLY someone said it."

4. **Give me 7 "hot take" content ideas that would start a conversation in my niche.** Controversial but true. The kind of stuff that gets comments.

5. **Write me a story-based post about why I started my business.** Make it emotional, real, and end with a lesson my audience can use.

---

## Client Outreach (land deals without being sleazy)

6. **Write me a cold DM to send to a potential client.** Keep it casual, not salesy. Like I'm reaching out as a human, not a pitch bot. Make it sound like me.

7. **Write a follow-up message for someone who ghosted me after showing interest.** Not desperate. Not passive-aggressive. Just cool and confident.

8. **Draft a proposal email for a potential client.** Include what I'd do for them, why it works, and a clear next step. Keep it under 200 words.

9. **Write me 3 different ways to ask for a testimonial from a happy client.** One casual DM, one email, one post-call script.

10. **Create a "warm outreach" template I can customize for any lead.** Something I can send to someone who engaged with my content but hasn't bought yet.

---

## Email & Newsletters (get opens, get clicks)

11. **Write me a welcome email for new subscribers.** Set the tone, tell them what to expect, and make them excited they signed up. Sound like me, not a corporation.

12. **Give me 5 email subject lines that would make MY audience open immediately.** Based on what they care about and what I sell.

13. **Write me a 3-email nurture sequence that turns a cold lead into a buyer.** Email 1: value. Email 2: story. Email 3: offer. All in my voice.

14. **Write me a "re-engagement" email for subscribers who haven't opened in 30 days.** Make it funny and honest. Not "we miss you" — something they'd actually want to read.

15. **Draft a launch announcement email for [my next offer/product/service].** Build hype, explain the value, and end with a clear CTA.

---

## Ad Copy & Sales (sell without sounding like an ad)

16. **Write me 3 variations of ad copy for [my product/service].** One emotional, one logical, one curiosity-driven. All under 150 words. All sound like me.

17. **Write me a VSL (video sales letter) script under 2 minutes.** Hook them in 5 seconds, explain the problem, present my solution, CTA. Conversational, not corporate.

18. **Give me 5 headlines for a landing page that sells [my offer].** Each one should make someone think "I need this" within 3 seconds.

19. **Write me a "reasons to buy" list for my product/service.** Not features — actual reasons a real person would pull out their credit card.

20. **Create a comparison post: what my audience is doing now vs. what they COULD be doing with my help.** Make the gap obvious and painful.

---

## Strategy & Planning (think bigger)

21. **Based on what you know about my business, what are the 3 highest-leverage things I should focus on this month?** Be specific and honest. Don't sugarcoat it.

22. **Audit my current setup. What am I missing?** Look at my tools, my audience, my goals — what gaps do you see? What would you add?

23. **Build me a 30-day content calendar.** Mix of content types — educational, personal stories, offers, engagement posts. Specific to my niche.

24. **What would a $10K/month version of my business look like?** Break down the math, the offers, the systems. Be realistic.

25. **If you were running my business for a week, what would you do differently?** Don't hold back. I want the honest version.

---

## Save Time (automate the boring stuff)

26. **Write me a client onboarding checklist.** Everything that should happen from "they paid" to "they're set up and happy." Based on my tools and workflow.

27. **Create a FAQ document for my business.** The 10 questions I get asked the most, with answers I can copy-paste to save time.

28. **Write me a SOP for [repetitive task I do weekly].** Step-by-step, so I could hand it off to someone or automate it.

29. **Build me an intake form — the questions I should ask every new client.** Based on what I do and who I serve. Nothing unnecessary.

30. **Write me a "what to post when you have no ideas" emergency list.** 10 plug-and-play content formats I can use any time I'm stuck. Specific to my business.
