# Business-in-a-Box Prompt Pack

10 mega-prompts that each build an ENTIRE system in one shot. One paste, one complete output. All personalized to your CLAUDE.md — your business, your audience, your voice.

These aren't prompts. They're blueprints. Each one builds something that would normally take you hours.

---

## 1. Complete 30-Day Content Calendar

```
Build me a complete 30-day content calendar for all my platforms. For each day, give me: the platform, the content type (educational, story, offer, engagement, behind-the-scenes), the topic, a hook, and a one-line description of the post. Mix up the formats — carousels, reels, stories, text posts, threads. Make sure every piece sounds like ME (check my CLAUDE.md voice) and speaks to MY audience. Include 2 offer posts per week, 3 educational, 1 personal story, and 1 engagement post. No generic filler — every single day should be something I'd actually want to post.
```

---

## 2. Client Onboarding Workflow

```
Build me a complete client onboarding workflow — everything that happens from "they just paid" to "they're fully set up and happy." Include: a welcome email (in my voice), an intake questionnaire (the exact questions I should ask), a kickoff checklist (what I need to prepare before we start), a "what to expect" doc I can send them, and a Week 1 action plan. Base everything on what I do, who I serve, and the tools I use. Make it feel personal, not corporate. I want my clients to feel like they made the best decision of their life the moment they onboard.
```

---

## 3. Sales Page Copy

```
Write me a complete sales page for my main offer. Include: a headline that stops the scroll, a subheadline that explains the value in one sentence, a "who this is for" section, 5-7 bullet point benefits (outcomes, not features), a "what you get" breakdown, 3 placeholder testimonial formats I can fill in, an FAQ section (5 common objections turned into questions with answers that sell), pricing section copy, and a strong CTA. Write the whole thing in my voice — check my CLAUDE.md. This should sound like ME selling, not a copywriter template. Make it conversational, direct, and impossible to say no to.
```

---

## 4. 5-Email Welcome Sequence

```
Write me a 5-email welcome sequence for new subscribers. Email 1: Welcome + set expectations + quick win (give them value immediately). Email 2: My story — why I do what I do (build connection). Email 3: Best piece of content/resource I've created (prove my value). Email 4: Common mistake my audience makes + how to fix it (teach). Email 5: Soft pitch for my offer (natural transition from value to sale). Each email should have: a subject line that gets opens, body copy in my voice (casual, not corporate), and a clear purpose. Keep each email under 300 words. My audience should feel like they're getting texts from a smart friend, not a newsletter.
```

---

## 5. Social Media Bio Pack

```
Write me optimized bios for every platform I use. For each one, include: the bio text (within character limits), a CTA, and a link suggestion. Platforms: Instagram (150 chars), TikTok (80 chars), Twitter/X (160 chars), LinkedIn (headline + about section), YouTube (channel description), and any other platforms from my CLAUDE.md. Each bio should clearly communicate: who I am, who I help, and why they should follow/connect. Match my voice — not generic "helping [audience] achieve [result]" templates. Make each one feel like ME. Include emoji strategically, not excessively.
```

---

## 6. Weekly Newsletter Template

```
Build me a reusable weekly newsletter template with sections I can fill in each week. Include: a catchy subject line formula (with 3 examples), an intro section (personal update or hot take — 2-3 sentences), a main content section (one valuable insight, tip, or lesson), a "tool/resource of the week" spotlight, a quick win (something they can implement today), and a sign-off with personality. Also write me one COMPLETE example issue using this template so I can see how it flows. Everything in my voice. This should take me 20 minutes to fill in each week, not 2 hours.
```

---

## 7. Lead Magnet + Landing Page Copy

```
Give me 3 lead magnet ideas that my specific audience would actually want (based on my CLAUDE.md). For the best one, build the full thing: an outline with 5-7 sections, a compelling title, a subtitle that sells the value, and the landing page copy to promote it. Landing page should include: headline, 3 bullet points of what they'll learn, a "who this is for" line, and an email opt-in CTA. Make the lead magnet something genuinely valuable — not a throwaway PDF. Something they'd feel guilty getting for free. Write everything in my voice.
```

---

## 8. Client Proposal Template

```
Build me a reusable client proposal template I can customize for each prospect. Include: an intro paragraph (personalized to the client — leave [brackets] for me to fill in), a "what I understand about your situation" section, my proposed solution (what I'll do, broken into phases or deliverables), timeline with milestones, pricing section (with 2-3 tier options), what's included vs. not included, next steps to get started, and a short "about me" section. Write it in my voice — professional but still me. Not a stuffy corporate proposal. This should feel like a confident expert laying out exactly what they're gonna do and why it'll work. Include notes on what to customize per client.
```

---

## 9. FAQ Page (10 Questions + Answers)

```
Write me a complete FAQ page for my business. Give me 10 questions that my audience actually asks (or thinks but doesn't ask), with answers that: address the concern honestly, reframe objections into benefits, and subtly sell without being pushy. Include questions about: pricing/investment, timeline/results, "is this right for me?", how it works, what makes me different, and common fears. Each answer should be 2-4 sentences — concise, confident, in my voice. These should handle objections before they become reasons not to buy. Don't write corporate FAQ garbage — write answers that sound like me talking to them directly.
```

---

## 10. Competitor Analysis Report

```
Analyze 3 competitors in my space (pick 3 that my audience would also consider based on my CLAUDE.md). For each one, break down: what they do well, what they do poorly, their positioning, their pricing (if visible), their content strategy, and their audience. Then give me: the gaps they're ALL missing that I can own, 3 angles I can use to differentiate myself, specific content ideas that attack their weak spots, and a one-paragraph "why choose me over them" statement I can use in my marketing. Be brutally honest about their strengths — but show me exactly where I win.
```
