## Phase 2: Information Gathering (Day 0-3)

### Intake Form Questions

Send these as a form or questionnaire. Get everything you need BEFORE the kickoff call so you're not wasting time on basics.

**Business Basics:**
1. What's your business name and website URL?
2. What do you sell? (Products, services, or both)
3. Who is your ideal customer? (Be as specific as possible — age, gender, income, location, interests)
4. What's your current monthly revenue? (Rough range is fine)
5. What's your monthly budget for [YOUR SERVICE — e.g., "ad spend" / "marketing" / "content"]?

**Current State:**
6. What marketing are you currently doing? What's working? What's not?
7. What tools/platforms are you currently using? (List everything — Shopify, Mailchimp, Meta Ads, etc.)
8. Do you have brand guidelines, logos, fonts, or brand colors? (If yes, share a link or file)
9. Who are your top 3 competitors?
10. What have you tried before that didn't work?

**Goals & Expectations:**
11. What does success look like for you in 90 days?
12. What's the #1 thing you want from working with me?
13. How do you prefer to communicate? (Email, Slack, text, Loom, etc.)
14. How often do you want updates? (Weekly, bi-weekly, as-needed)
15. Anything else I should know?

## Phase 3: Kickoff Call (Day 3-7)

### Pre-Call Prep (for you)

- [ ] Review intake form responses
- [ ] Look at their website, social media, and any existing marketing
- [ ] Check out their competitors (from question #9)
- [ ] Draft initial strategy notes / recommendations
- [ ] Prepare kickoff call agenda (below)

### Kickoff Call Agenda (30-45 min)

**1. Introductions & Vibe Check (5 min)**
- Quick personal connection — keep it human
- Confirm communication preferences
- "What are you most excited about? What are you most worried about?"

**2. Review Their Goals (10 min)**
- Walk through their intake form responses
- Clarify anything that was vague
- Align on what "success" means — specific numbers, not vibes
- Agree on 90-day milestones

**3. Present Your Plan (15 min)**
- Share your initial strategy / approach
- Walk through the phases and timeline
- Explain what you need from them and when
- Set expectations on what they'll see and when

**4. Access & Logistics (10 min)**
- Get access to everything you need:
  - [ ] Ad accounts (Meta Business Manager, Google Ads, etc.)
  - [ ] Social media accounts
  - [ ] Website backend (Shopify, WordPress, etc.)
  - [ ] Email platform (Klaviyo, Mailchimp, etc.)
  - [ ] Analytics (Google Analytics, etc.)
  - [ ] Brand assets (logos, photos, videos)
  - [ ] Any other tools or platforms
- Confirm project management setup — where they'll see updates
- Confirm communication channel — where they'll reach you

**5. Next Steps (5 min)**
- Recap action items for both sides
- Confirm next check-in date
- "Any questions before we get started?"

## Red Flags to Watch For

Things that signal the onboarding needs attention:

- Client hasn't filled out intake form after 3 days — send a reminder
- Client hasn't given access after kickoff — follow up same day
- Client is unresponsive for 5+ days — call them directly
- Scope creep starting in kickoff call — redirect to the proposal
- Client expectations don't match proposal — address immediately, not later
