## Your Content Pillars

Before generating a calendar, define your 4-5 content pillars. These are the recurring themes everything falls under.

| Pillar | What It Covers | Example Post |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| **Educate** | Tips, how-tos, frameworks your audience needs | "3 things killing your ad performance right now" |
| **Story** | Personal experiences, wins, failures, behind-the-scenes | "I almost quit my business last March. Here's what happened." |
| **Sell** | Direct offers, testimonials, case studies, CTAs | "My client went from $5K to $22K/mo in 60 days. Here's what we did." |
| **Engage** | Questions, polls, debates, "this or that" | "Hot take: email marketing > social media. Fight me." |
| **Personality** | Who you are outside of business — humor, opinions, life | "Things I've learned running a business from my couch" |

## Weekly Posting Schedule

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Pillar | Notes |
|-----|----------|-------------|--------|-------|
| **Monday** | Instagram (Reel) | Educational tip | Educate | Start the week with value. Quick win they can use today. |
| **Tuesday** | TikTok | Story / BTS | Story | Raw, unfiltered. Phone camera energy. |
| **Wednesday** | Instagram (Carousel) | Framework / How-to | Educate | Save-worthy. The kind of post people screenshot. |
| **Thursday** | TikTok | Hot take / Opinion | Engage | Controversial but true. Gets comments. |
| **Friday** | Instagram (Story series) | Soft sell / Case study | Sell | Show results. Let the work speak. |
| **Saturday** | TikTok | Personality / Humor | Personality | Light, fun, relatable. |
| **Sunday** | Instagram (Post) | Engagement / Question | Engage | Low effort, high interaction. |

**YouTube (if applicable):** 1 long-form video per week, posted [DAY]. Topic ties to the week's educational theme but goes deeper.

## Caption Starters (Swipe These)

**Educational:**
- "Most [audience] don't know this, but..."
- "Here's the exact process I use for [thing]"
- "3 things I wish someone told me about [topic]"
- "The difference between [thing A] and [thing B] is..."

**Story:**
- "I almost [dramatic moment]. Here's what I learned."
- "Last year I was [old situation]. Now I'm [new situation]. Here's what changed."
- "A client texted me this morning and said..."

**Sell:**
- "This is what happens when [your service] meets [their problem]"
- "Before working with me: [pain]. After: [result]."
- "I have [X] spots left for [month]. Here's what you get."

**Engage:**
- "Hot take: [opinion]. Agree or disagree?"
- "What's the one thing you'd change about your business right now?"
- "Tell me your [niche] and I'll tell you your biggest problem"
